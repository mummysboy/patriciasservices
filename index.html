Our Story

Patricia's Services is a locally owned, kama‘āina-run cleaning company built on a simple belief: every home and business on O‘ahu deserves to feel fresh, cared for, and truly clean. With over 20 years of hands-on experience in professional cleaning, we treat every space with the same attention to detail we'd give our own.

From busy families and vacation-rental hosts to offices and storefronts, our clients stay with us because we show up on time, communicate clearly, and stand behind every job with our same-day satisfaction guarantee. We're proud to serve our island community — and to do it with genuine aloha.
